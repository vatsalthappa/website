We are seeking a talented and experienced Compensation Analyst to join our growing team and help build the foundation for our compensation programs and processes. You will be responsible for developing and implementing comprehensive compensation strategies that support our rapid growth and ensure competitive and equitable compensation practices. You will partner closely with leadership, HR, and finance teams to create scalable compensation programs that attract, retain, and motivate top talent.

You thrive in a fast-paced startup environment and have a proven track record of building and scaling compensation functions. You understand the unique challenges of growing teams in emerging technology markets and can effectively balance strategic thinking with hands-on execution.

Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement comprehensive compensation strategies and programs
  • Conduct detailed market analysis using tools like Mercer, Radford, and Pave to ensure competitive compensation
  • Collaborate with HR and recruiting teams, guiding them through salary offers and job leveling conversations
  • Develop models and scenarios to help leadership visualize compensation strategies aligned with business objectives
  • Update job architectures and compensation structures as we scale, ensuring fairness and transparency
  • Analyze compensation data and provide insights to leadership for data-driven decision making
  • Manage equity compensation programs and private company valuation processes (409A)
  • Ensure compliance with compensation laws and regulations
  • Build and maintain relationships with external compensation vendors and partners
  • Keep a pulse on market trends and proactively recommend adjustments to stay competitive

Background

  • 3-5 years of experience in compensation, finance, or analytical roles in high-growth technology companies
  • Expert-level spreadsheet skills with experience creating pivot tables, complex models, and scenario analyses
  • Hands-on experience with salary benchmarking surveys like Mercer, Radford, and Pave
  • Strong grasp of equity compensation, private company valuation, and option pricing (409A)
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with attention to detail
  • Data-driven approach with experience using compensation analytics to inform decisions
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-growth environment
  • Strong communication skills with the ability to translate complex data into clear insights
  • Experience with compensation program design and implementation
  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Business, Human Resources, or related field preferred
  • CCP (Certified Compensation Professional) certification preferred

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and equity package
  • Comprehensive health, dental, and vision benefits
  • Flexible work arrangements and remote-friendly culture
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Collaborative and innovative work environment
  • Opportunity to shape the future of compensation at AIGETAI
  • Access to cutting-edge compensation tools and technologies

Note: This role requires experience in compensation analysis within the technology sector
