Who are we? +

AIGETAI is a creative AI platform designed for creators, designers, and teams. We empower you to create stunning visuals, craft compelling stories, and generate professional motion graphics—all powered by advanced AI technology. Our mission is to democratize creative tools and help you bring your ideas to life without limits. Dream Machine app on iOS from the App Store. Simply search for "Dream Machine".

How do we start working with new clients? +

Getting started with AIGETAI is simple and straightforward. First, you'll sign up for an account on our platform. Next, our onboarding team will guide you through a discovery session to understand your creative needs and goals. We'll then set up your project workspace, provide access to our tools, and assign a dedicated account manager to support your journey. You can start creating immediately after setup, with access to tutorials, templates, and best practices to help you succeed.

How do clients track their project? +

Our platform provides comprehensive project tracking and monitoring tools. Clients can access a real-time dashboard that shows project progress, milestones, and deliverables. You'll receive regular status updates, progress reports, and can communicate with your team through our integrated collaboration tools. All project files, versions, and iterations are organized in one centralized location with full history and revision tracking for transparency and accountability.

How do we ensure quality in our projects? +

Quality assurance is at the core of everything we do. We employ a rigorous multi-stage quality process including AI refinement, expert review, and client feedback loops. Our advanced algorithms ensure pixel-perfect results, while our team of experienced creatives provides manual oversight on all deliverables. We conduct comprehensive testing, maintain strict quality standards, and continuously iterate based on client feedback until you're completely satisfied with the results.
